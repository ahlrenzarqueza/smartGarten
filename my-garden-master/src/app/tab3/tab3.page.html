How to use your SmartGarden

Follow these three easy steps!
1

Fill the tub with water up to the mark. Add prescribed amount of fertilizer.
2

Put the pump in the water. The inseminated and germinated plant pads are then placed on the grids.
3

Plug the plug into the wall socket and set the desired configuration of your SmartGarden directly here in the app!
